Welcome to Commerce

It only takes a few minutes to get started with the DotNetNuke Commerce module. Follow the steps below to configure the module.

  1. Choose Your Payment Options
    You will need to select at least one payment method before you can start selling. Payment methods can be defined under the Settings section in the Commerce Control Panel.
  2. Create Your First Product
    Select products from the Commerce Control Panel and click the Create Product button.
  3. Complete a Test Purchase
    Make sure everything is configured properly by completing a test purchase on your own.
